April Challenge

Sustaining alumni giving is vitally important to the health of Punahou School. In order to achieve this, several alumni donors are challenging fellow alumni (who are not in a reunion year) to support the Punahou Annual Fund during the month of April. Every gift makes a difference. And your participation is the key to our success. The goal is to raise $50,000 in 30 days for the Annual Fund. Please consider investing in our Punahou students, faculty and staff through a gift to the Annual Fund.
